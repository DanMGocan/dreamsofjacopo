How SlidePull Works

SlidePull makes it easy to share specific slides from your presentations with your audience.

Whether you're a presenter, educator, or business professional, our platform helps you create QR codes that link directly to the slides you want to share.

1. Upload

Upload your PowerPoint presentation to your account. We'll convert it to a PDF format that's easy to view on any device.

2. Select

Choose which slides you want to include in your set. You can create multiple sets from a single presentation.

3. Share

Generate a QR code for your slide set and share it with your audience. They can scan it to view your slides instantly.

Key Features

Presentation Slots

Upload your presentations to dedicated slots in your account. Free users get 1 slot, while premium users get unlimited slots.

Slide Selection

Choose exactly which slides you want to include in each set. You can create multiple sets from a single presentation.

QR Code Generation

Generate QR codes for each slide set. Your audience can scan these codes to view your slides on any device.

Mobile-Friendly Viewing

Your slides are optimized for viewing on any device, including smartphones and tablets.
