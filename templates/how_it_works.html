How SlidePull Works

SlidePull converts your PowerPoint presentations into shareable slides with QR codes.

Our platform is designed for educators, presenters, and professionals who need to share specific slides with their audience quickly and securely.

1. Upload

Upload your PowerPoint presentation to your account. We'll convert it to a PDF format that's viewable on any device.

2. Select

Choose which slides you want to include in your set. You can create multiple sets from a single presentation.

3. Share

Generate a QR code for your slide set and share it with your audience. They can scan it to view your slides instantly.

Technology & Security

Azure Cloud Infrastructure

All SlidePull operations run on secure Azure Virtual Machines. Your presentations and generated QR codes are stored on Azure Servers with enterprise-grade security.

In-Memory Conversion

Your presentations are processed in-memory, ensuring that your content is never written to disk during conversion, enhancing security and privacy.

File Retention Policy

Files that haven't been accessed or downloaded in 60 days are automatically deleted to maintain system efficiency and ensure your storage allocation is used effectively.

Usage Analytics

All users have access to download analytics, allowing you to track how many times your presentations and slide sets have been accessed.
